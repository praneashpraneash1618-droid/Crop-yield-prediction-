How it works

From raw field signal to a decision you can act on today.

One continuous pipeline runs every day of the season — no separate tools to reconcile, no stale reports.

STAGE 01

Sense

Daily satellite passes, soil moisture probes, weather stations and optional drone flyovers feed raw signal into Terravue continuously, at sub-field resolution.

STAGE 02

Predict

Crop-specific ML models trained on your field's own history forecast yield, water demand and disease pressure zone-by-zone, updated every 24 hours.

STAGE 03

Recommend

The system converts predictions into specific actions — where to irrigate, how much nitrogen to apply, which block to scout first — ranked by expected impact.

STAGE 04

Verify

Actual harvest and sensor data feed back into the model, so every season it understands your soil, your microclimate, and your crop a little better.

The platform

Everything a field team needs to act early, in one place.

Built for agronomists and growers, not data scientists — every insight ends in a recommendation, not a chart.

Yield forecasting

Zone-level yield predictions from planting through harvest, refined daily as new imagery and weather data arrive.

Irrigation optimization

Soil-moisture-aware watering schedules by zone, cutting water use without risking yield during dry spells.

Pest & disease early warning

Risk models flag likely outbreak zones 7–10 days ahead using canopy signal, humidity and regional pressure data.

Soil health mapping

Sub-field maps of moisture, organic matter and compaction, so input decisions match what's actually in the ground.

Weather-adjusted planning

Planting, spraying and harvest windows re-ranked automatically as forecasts shift, with clear reasoning behind each change.

Resource savings tracker

Running tally of water, fertilizer and fuel saved against a season baseline, in units and in cost.
